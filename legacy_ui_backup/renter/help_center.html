Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay my rent online?

You can pay your rent through the portal by following these steps:

  1. Go to the Payments tab in your dashboard
  2. Click on "Pay Now" for the current month's rent
  3. Select your payment method (bank account or credit card)
  4. Enter the payment amount and confirm
  5. You'll receive a confirmation email with your receipt

Note: Payments may take 1-3 business days to process.

What should I do in case of a maintenance emergency?

For maintenance emergencies that require immediate attention (such as flooding, no heat in winter, or electrical issues):

  • During business hours (9AM-5PM): Call the property manager at (307) 228-4667
  • After hours: Call the 24/7 emergency line at (307) 228-4667
  • For life-threatening emergencies: Always call 911 first

Please also submit a maintenance request through the portal for documentation purposes.

How do I request a copy of my lease agreement?

You can download your lease agreement at any time from the Documents tab in your portal. If you need a signed copy or have trouble accessing it:

  1. Go to the Documents tab
  2. Click on "Lease Agreement" to download
  3. If the document is not available, contact support@sotelomanage.com
  4. Allow 1-2 business days for processing

What is the late fee policy for rent payments?

Our late fee policy is as follows:

  • Rent is due on the 1st of each month
  • A 5-day grace period is provided (rent is considered late after the 5th)
  • Late fee: $75 if paid between the 6th and 10th
  • Additional $10 per day after the 10th
  • Maximum late fee: $150

We recommend setting up auto-pay to avoid late fees.

How do I update my contact information?

To update your contact information:

  1. Go to "My Profile" from the sidebar
  2. Edit your contact details in the Personal Information section
  3. Click "Save Changes" to update
  4. Important: Updating your email or phone may require verification

Please ensure your contact information is always current for emergency notifications.

What should I do if I'm locked out of my apartment?

If you're locked out during business hours (9AM-5PM):

  • Contact the property manager at (307) 228-4667
  • A staff member can let you in during office hours

If you're locked out after hours:

  • Call the 24/7 emergency line at (307) 228-4667
  • There is a $50 service fee for after-hours lockouts
  • Consider contacting a locksmith at your own expense

We recommend keeping a spare key with a trusted neighbor or family member.
